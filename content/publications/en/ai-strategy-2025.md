## The tipping point

2025 is a turning point. Generative AI is no longer a technological promise: it is a strategic lever that redraws competitive advantages across every sector.

Companies that integrate AI today create a measurable gap with those who hesitate. It is not a budget issue. It is a methodology issue.

## Three mistakes to avoid

- **The tool rush**: buying licenses without strategy is wasting capital.
- **Waiting for regulation**: the AI Act is not an excuse to stay still. It is already partially applicable.
- **Executive silence**: without business understanding of AI, decisions get delegated to technical teams without a global vision.
