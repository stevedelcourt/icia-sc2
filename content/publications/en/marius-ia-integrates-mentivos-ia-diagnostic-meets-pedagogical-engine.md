This is not a training supply problem. It is a pedagogical architecture problem. And that is exactly what MentivisOS solves.

Starting this month, Marius IA integrates MentivisOS into its support framework. This is not a tool announcement. It is a coherence decision.

### What MentivisOS does, and what it is not

MentivisOS is not an LMS. This point deserves to be stated clearly, because the confusion is frequent and costly. An LMS distributes content. It stores, it organizes, it tracks. What it does not do is determine whether the distributed content actually closes the skill gap of the person receiving it. You can complete a module without having progressed a single centimeter. The training market knows this. Few admit it.

MentivisOS is built around a different logic. You declare an objective, a starting situation, time constraints. In under a minute, the system produces a complete diagnostic: coverage score, residual risk, estimated duration, identified modules, learning order. Not a list. A reasoned sequence. Then the program is built module by module, theory, practice, assessment, with an embedded pedagogical assistant that stays within the subject scope, without drifting.

What MentivisOS produces is the exact program a given person needs. No more. No less.

### Why Marius IA integrates this tool

Marius IA supports businesses, liberal professions, educational institutions, and local authorities. Each arrives with a different AI maturity, different professions, different rhythms. The diagnostic that Marius IA performs at the start of a mission produces precisely this mapping: who knows what, who needs what, and by when.

The question that immediately followed was: and now, how do we build skills in a way adapted to each profile, without drowning them in a catalog, without losing them along the way?

MentivisOS is the answer to this question. It transforms the diagnostic into an operational program. It gives body to the roadmap. It allows Marius IA to not stop at prescription but to support execution with a tool designed for that purpose.

This is the logical continuity of what Marius IA has been doing from the start: not selling a ready-made solution, but building what each organization truly needs.

### What this concretely changes for clients

For businesses and teams that Marius IA supports in transformation, MentivisOS integrates without friction. Three access modes are available: direct access per learner, operational in 24 hours without any infrastructure to deploy; an enterprise license with customized professional frameworks and a Mentivis onboarding; or an API connection with existing tools, LMS, HRIS, or ATS.

Concretely, an HR department that has ordered an AI diagnostic can, in the same breath, deploy individualized learning paths for each employee identified as a priority. Without having to choose a separate training provider. Without waiting for the next group session. Without buying an additional LMS.

For training organizations and schools that work with Mentivis, MentivisOS offers something even more precise: fine-grained visibility into gaps, the end of a one-size-fits-all curriculum applied to everyone, and the construction of operational skill frameworks in just a few days.

### A platform developed by Mentivis, integrated into a unified logic

MentivisOS is developed by Mentivis, the education consulting firm of which Marius IA is the specialized AI strategy arm. This proximity is not a coincidence. It ensures that the tool is designed from the same principles as the support: start from the real need, produce something measurable, adapt to what exists rather than imposing an additional layer.

The fact that both structures share the same pedagogical foundations enables an integration that is not cosmetic. The Marius IA diagnostic and the MentivisOS engine speak the same language. What is identified upstream can be addressed directly downstream.

### Going further

MentivisOS is available today for Marius IA corporate clients. There is no standard demo. Each presentation is built around a real case from the organization in question, handled live.

For organizations already being supported by Marius IA, the question of integrating MentivisOS into the existing framework can be raised directly at the next follow-up meeting.

For those discovering Marius IA through this channel: the entry point remains the same. A diagnostic, a roadmap, and now an engine to execute it.
