# homepage.gouvernance.title

The Institut Collectif de l'IA (ICIA) is an independent non-profit association.

# homepage.gouvernance.body.2

# homepage.gouvernance.body.3

# homepage.gouvernance.mentivis.title

# homepage.gouvernance.mentivis.desc

# homepage.gouvernance.mentivis.note
