ICIA launches the Impact Program for AI skills development

May 10, 2026

A training program designed to support citizens, professionals and organizations facing AI-driven transformations.

The Institut Collectif de l'IA (ICIA) announces the launch of its Impact Program, a skills development initiative designed to support those facing AI-driven transformations.

Five progressive modules

The Impact Program is structured around five progressive modules, with no prerequisites:

  1. Understand without suffering — establish a shared vocabulary, distinguish reality from fantasy
  2. Master everyday tools — write, research, organize with AI
  3. Exercise judgment — recognize errors, biases, generated information
  4. Understand what you give up — personal data, consent, GDPR
  5. Participate in the debate — AI and work, democracy, sovereignty

A public interest program

Developed by ICIA with Mentivis as the educational operator, the Impact Program is intended for associative, educational, cultural and territorial structures.

Training sessions are offered in-person in Marseille (Campus Cyber.IA Euromed), remotely and in hybrid formats.
