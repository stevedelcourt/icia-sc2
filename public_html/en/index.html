Notre mission

Our mission

AI is no longer just for engineers, labs or large corporations. It is progressively redefining expected skills, professions, access to information, creation, automated decisions, and economic and democratic balances.

Making AI understandable

Explaining technologies, their limits, their real uses and their social implications.

Building skills

Enabling individuals and organizations to understand, use, and question AI tools.

Reducing divides

Limiting gaps in access, understanding, and opportunities linked to AI adoption.

Fostering informed debate

Creating spaces for dialogue among citizens, professionals, institutions, educators and decision-makers.

ICIA

Who we are

The Institut Collectif de l'IA (ICIA) is a French non-profit association dedicated to the collective challenges of artificial intelligence.

Our conviction is simple: the AI transition will be neither purely technical nor purely economic. It is also cultural, educational, social and democratic.

That is why we develop initiatives enabling individuals and organizations to understand the ongoing transformations and take part in them.

Modèle

An association model supported by specialized operators

The Institut Collectif de l'IA (ICIA) is an independent non-profit association.

The association defines its mission, orientations, priorities and public interest programs around artificial intelligence, skills and societal transformations.

For certain initiatives, ICIA relies on specialized partners capable of ensuring the design, engineering and operational deployment of programs.

Frequently asked questions

The Institut Collectif de l'IA (ICIA) is a French non-profit association dedicated to the collective challenges of artificial intelligence.

Citizens, professionals, organizations, public actors and the educational world. Our programs are designed for everyone who has to navigate AI in their daily lives.

ICIA programs are funded through voluntary contributions, public grants, and partnerships with foundations and companies committed to the public interest.

In-person in Marseille (Campus Cyber.IA Euromed), remotely, and in hybrid formats. Sessions can also be organized at your premises.

Contact us via the contact form to join a program, propose a partnership, or contribute to the association's activities.

ICIA defines the programmatic orientations and quality requirements. It relies on a network of specialized operational partners in training engineering and educational deployment for the concrete implementation of programs.

Born in Marseille. Built for France.

The Institut Collectif de l'IA is a national initiative, initially anchored in Marseille, in the same location as Mariusia.

Because AI is already transforming skills, education, work and access to information, its challenges cannot be reserved for a few specialized players or a few major cities.

ICIA champions a distributed approach, anchored in territories, connected to field realities and open to all public, educational, economic and associative actors.
