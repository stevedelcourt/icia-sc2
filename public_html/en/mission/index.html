Mission

Our mission

Making AI understandable

Explaining technologies, their limits, their real uses and their social implications.

Building skills

Enabling individuals and organizations to understand, use, and question AI tools.

Reducing divides

Limiting gaps in access, understanding, and opportunities linked to AI adoption.

Fostering informed debate

Creating spaces for dialogue among citizens, professionals, institutions, educators and decision-makers.

AI is no longer just for engineers, labs or large corporations. It is progressively redefining expected skills, professions, access to information, creation, automated decisions, and economic and democratic balances.

Faced with this shift, ICIA pursues a clear mission: making AI understandable, building skills, reducing divides and fostering informed debate.
